Featured Recipe: Sanjiv

Sanjiv is a traditional dish that brings warmth and flavor to your table.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water.
  2. In a pot, bring water to a boil.
  3. Add rice, salt, and oil.
  4. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Fluff with a fork and serve hot.

Tips:

  • For extra flavor, add spices like cumin or cardamom.
  • Serve with your favorite curry or side dish.
  • Garnish with fresh herbs for a vibrant touch.

Enjoy your meal!
